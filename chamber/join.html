Benefits

Bronze Membership

Benefits
  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Monthly business insights newsletter.
  • Exclusive discounts on business training and development programs.
  • Access to premium business documents and templates.

Price: $50 per month.

Silver Membership

Benefits
  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Free participation in an annual business seminar or workshop.
  • Business promotion and advertising opportunities on the chamber's platform.
  • Personalized advice on business growth strategies.

Price: $100 per month.

Gold Membership

Benefits
  • All NP, Bronze, and Silver Membership benefits.
  • Unlimited access to all business-related events and conferences.
  • Priority consideration for business grants and sponsorships.
  • Advanced strategic business consultation with industry experts.
  • Prominent business advertising on the chamber's website and promotional materials.

Price: $200 per month.
